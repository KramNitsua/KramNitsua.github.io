CULTIVATING JOY MISSION

We are a Self Supported Ministry. Our dream is to bring people, nature and nurture together to cultivate healthy habits for physical, mental and spiritual well being. Our approach seeks to connect to the Creator through hands on  experience of a simpler sustainable lifestyle, personalized exercise strategies, meditation, prayer, organic gardening, crafting natural remedies, proper food preparation, storage and a focus on high density nutrition and detoxification. Our goal is to develop a working habitat that has a door open to the seeker for a short term or longer term visits. We intend to offer onsite lodging to welcome guests like you to visit  and participate in Cultivating Joy Mission's vision. We invite your inquiries, input and support!
